Computer Science

June 9, 2024

As a computer science student at North Eastern Mindanao State University, you have a plethora of exciting opportunities to enhance your learning and broaden your horizons. Participating in hackathons can be both fun and educational, offering a chance to collaborate and innovate with peers. Joining or starting a tech club can keep you updated with the latest technologies and provide a platform for group projects and networking. Contributing to open-source projects on platforms like GitHub can give you real-world experience and a chance to collaborate with developers worldwide. Additionally, working on personal projects, such as developing mobile apps, creating websites, or exploring AI and machine learning, can significantly boost your skills and portfolio. Enhancing your knowledge through online courses and certifications on platforms like Coursera, edX, and Udemy can give you an edge in specialized areas. Lastly, attending tech conferences and meetups can expose you to industry trends and help you build a professional network,making your time at university both enriching and enjoyable.
